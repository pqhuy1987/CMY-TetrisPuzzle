CMY is short for Cyan, Magenta and Yellow. These are the three basic colors used in modern day color printing. Mixing these colors is the objective of the game.
Cyan and magenta make blue, cyan and yellow make green, magenta and yellow make red. Together, they make black.
Rather play with the familiar red, green and blue your computer uses?
Or do you prefer the old fashioned red, yellow and blue color model? It's your choice!
Compete against your friends for the highest score. With full game center integration, it's you versus the world!
For the true die-hards, we also have a few achievements to obtain. Beware: all of them play hard to get.
